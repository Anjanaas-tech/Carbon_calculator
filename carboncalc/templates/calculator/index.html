Carbon Footprint Calculator

The Carbon Footprint Calculator helps individuals estimate the environmental impact of their daily activities, including household energy consumption and food choices. By inputting data on electricity, gas, water usage, and food intake, users can calculate their carbon emissions in kilograms of CO₂ per day. This interactive tool raises awareness about sustainable living and provides actionable insights to reduce carbon footprints, promoting a more eco-friendly lifestyle.
